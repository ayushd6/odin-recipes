Golgappa

Description

This is a North Indian classic that I grew up eating. It involves fried puffs of semolina, stuffed with chickpeas, potatoes, spices, and filled with a spicy water.

Ingredients

  • Semolina Puffs
  • Cilantro-Mint-Chili Water
  • Chickpeas
  • Potatoes

Steps

  1. Get everything out
  2. Put it all together
